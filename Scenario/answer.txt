AMAZON PRODUCT METADATA USE CASES:

1. Search + Filters
   - Enables filtering by size, color, brand, category etc.
   - Helps products appear in relevant search queries.

2. Personalization
   - Shows results that match user's history
   - Example: Customer mostly buys Slim-Fit Navy Shirts  rank similar items higher

3. Demand Forecasting
   - When launching a new shirt with no sales history, forecasting uses metadata similarity (brand, color, fit) to predict demand
   - Solves the cold-start problem

4. Inventory Planning
   - Warehouse stocking decisions based on metadata trends
   - Example: Men Slim Cotton sells more in Bangalore

5. Recommendation Systems
   - Used for "Similar products" suggestions
   - Used for "Frequently viewed together" recommendations

6. Catalog Consistency
   - Maintains product grouping
   - De-duplicates products
   - Maintains variant structure

7. Supply Chain Optimization
   - Helps route items to correct fulfillment centers
   - Separates Men's clothing vs Women's clothing etc.
